About

Golden Gym is the largest chain of clubs or fitness centers in the world. The first gymnasium was established in 1965 in Venice Beach, California. It includes a range of sports equipment, tools and personal trainers to assist clients. It is headquartered in Irving, Texas. Joe Gold opened the first Gold's Gym in August 1965, in Venice Beach, California,long before the modern day health club existed. Featuring homemade equipment and dubbed "the Mecca of bodybuilding", it was frequented by Arnold Schwarzenegger and Dave Draper.
